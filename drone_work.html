DEUS EX MACHINA

DEUS : BALI LIFESTYLE

Deus Ex Machina is a restaurant and shop for surf and motorcycle enthusiasts. Located central of Canggu Bali , Deus has become an iconic landmark for travellers. Weekly entertainment, dj's, sunday session bands are a popular. And great food menu of international and local dishes. It was a privelage filming Deus and seeing how hard the staff work due to a high number of customers they receive on a basis.

OCEAN YOGA FESTIVAL

YOGA FOR A CAUSE

Ocean Yoga Festival charity event was held in Amed Bali Oct 7 to Oct 14 2018. This free event showcased classes of yoga, dance, music, meditation, martial arts, performance arts, personal health & well being. Classes was spread over 6 venues along the beautiful coastline of Amed east Bali. Donations were given to local charity Suluwesi & Lombok to name a few. I was the only videographer during this week so it was a challenge taking on this massive task.

Go on Youtube !
